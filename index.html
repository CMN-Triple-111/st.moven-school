Our Mission & Values

At St. Movens, our mission is to nurture intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and strong moral character in a supportive Zambian context.

We envision a community of learners who are prepared not just for examinations, but for life, equipped with both academic excellence and practical skills. Our core values include **Integrity**, **Growth**, **Community**, and **Excellence**.
